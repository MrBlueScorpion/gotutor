STUDENT FAQ

What is the average hourly rate?

Tutor’s hourly rate depends on the distance travelled and the number of students attending a session. We encourage students to contact a few tutors in order to compare their hourly rate. In most circumstances better tutor charges more fees.

What if I don't like the tutor?

Students can search the tutor and click Review a Tutor button.

Do you have tutors that specialise in helping children with learning disabilities?

Refer to tutor’s detailed descriptions for special requirements.

How do I pay the tutor? What forms of payment do you accept?

Most tutors prefer cash and some tutors offer first lesson free.
